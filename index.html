About Us

With over 20 years in the industry, LUMINA ELECTRICS have the experience and versatility to carry out first rate electrical services across Peterborough and surrounding areas. LUMINA Electrics has worked on both private and many planned work frameworks delivering thousands of installations per year. These include electrical compliance (EICRs), Fire detection (Smoke/Heat/Carbon monoxide alarms), full/partial rewiring of installations on kitchen and bathroom streams, emergency lighting and electric heating to name a few. With team of qualified and highly experienced tradesmen, we can carry out electrical projects of all sizes. We are registered with the NAPIT as an approved contractor. We are fully qualified electricians with up-to-date health and safety training.
